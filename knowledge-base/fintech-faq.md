# Fintech Knowledge Base - Sample Content

## Account Management

### Opening an Account
**Q: How do I open a new account?**  
**A**: To open a new account with us, you can:
1. Apply online through our website
2. Visit any of our branch locations
3. Call our customer service at 1-800-FINTECH

Required documents:
- Government-issued photo ID
- Social Security number
- Proof of address (utility bill or bank statement)
- Initial deposit (minimum $25 for savings, $100 for checking)

### Account Types
**Checking Accounts**
- Standard Checking: No monthly fee with $500 minimum balance
- Premium Checking: Advanced features, $15/month or waive with $2,500 balance
- Student Checking: Free for students under 25

**Savings Accounts**
- Basic Savings: 0.50% APY, $25 minimum opening deposit
- High-Yield Savings: 2.25% APY, $1,000 minimum balance
- Money Market: 2.75% APY, $5,000 minimum balance

### Account Security
**Q: How do I protect my account from fraud?**  
**A**: Follow these security best practices:
- Never share your login credentials
- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Monitor your account regularly
- Report suspicious activity immediately
- Use secure networks for online banking

## Transaction Support

### Online Transfers
**Internal Transfers**
- Between your accounts: Instant and free
- Daily limit: $25,000
- Monthly limit: $100,000

**External Transfers**
- To other banks: 1-3 business days
- Fee: $3 per transfer
- Daily limit: $10,000

### Wire Transfers
**Domestic Wires**
- Same day processing if submitted before 3 PM EST
- Fee: $25 outgoing, $15 incoming
- Required information: Recipient bank routing number, account number, name

**International Wires**
- 1-5 business days depending on destination
- Fee: $45 outgoing, $20 incoming
- Additional correspondent bank fees may apply

### Payment Services
**Bill Pay**
- Free online bill pay service
- Schedule one-time or recurring payments
- Electronic payments arrive in 1-2 business days
- Check payments take 3-5 business days

**Mobile Payments**
- Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay supported
- Instant notifications for all transactions
- Contactless payment options available
